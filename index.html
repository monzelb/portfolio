About me

I am a software developer who loves to create, collaborate, and build useful things. I am inspired by great ideas, user-centered design, and efficient code, and prioritize these in my work.

A long time practitioner of mindfulness, and former Buddhist monk, I aspire to build a compassionate, mindful, inclusive environment wherever I work.

I am a believer that the power of technology can be leveraged to create a better world.

Buddhist monk

I helped launch this sustainable food-tech startup which serves gourmet plant-based meals in reusable containers. Being involved in a startup from its beginning taught me a lot about business operations, and piqued my interest in tech.

Non-profit management

I had the good fortune to serve as the manager of their center in San Francisco, where I managed web content (Wordpress), email marketing campaigns (Constant Contact), social media, accounting, and community organizing.

2017

Bootcamp

After learning software development on my own for a year and a half I realized that I wanted to plunge in deeper and signed up for General Assembly's 12 week web development bootcamp, where I learned full-stack development in Javascript and Ruby on Rails, and developed the skill of picking up languages and frameworks very rapidly.

2017

I worked with a team of developers building a crowd-funding app for Berkeley Public Schools, which allows teachers to apply for grants for their classes, which anyone can donate to through the app.

2017-present

Agency Web Developer
